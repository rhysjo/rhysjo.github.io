[1] - flex-grow: 1 | [5] - flex-grow:2

Allows the flex item to grow and take up any available space in the container proprtionally to other flex items.
Number 5 will take up twice as much space as Number 1

[1] - flex-shrink: 1 | [5] - flex-shrink:2

Allows the flex item to shrink proprtionally when there is not enough space in the container.
Number 5 will shrink in size, twice as fast as Number 1.
Setting the shrink to 0 will prevent the element from shrinking down.
